Taj Mahal Review x

June 2015

One poem appeared in Taj Mahal Review, June 2015 and is a poem that is taken from Ali's book - Rose & Ashes. The poem searches for an alternative reality and chooses to live in it than remain in one which is real. The rose tinted glasses create a reality that seems bearable than the real one. The poem considers the possibility of living in this world by keeping alive the imaginative reality.
