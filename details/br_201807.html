Reliving Memories - Real And Imagined

In the two books of poetry Rahman's and Daruwalla's, there is a preoccupation with time, the fluidity of it and how one interprets it in their own manner. The concept of what constitutes as home changes accordingly. Home takes on different meanings for Rahman with the description of familiar sights, smells and people that define him as well as how he tends to look back whereas for Daruwalla, the concept of home has changed.
