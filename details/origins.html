Origins x

2012

Origins was published by Amitabh Mitra- The Poets Printery, South Africa in 2012.

The book talks of the origins of feelings, of relationship and of one's relation with the world. Be it in terms of personal, political or social. It explores the theme of how one chooses a particular set of ideas and ideals to stick to and to what extent are they affected by one's origins. To what extent are the origins embedded in our psyche to make us take certain decisions and how they suffocate/ release us with/from its hold.

The book is available with the publisher.
