Muse India #37 x

2011

The four poems were published in Muse India Issue 37, 2011 and bring out relationships through different ways and circumstances. A sense of loss combines with a sense of longing which translates into a sense of euphoria. The transient nature of the emotions and how a particular emotion gives way to another is what the sequential order in which the poems are laid down tries to achieve.
