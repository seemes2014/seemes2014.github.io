A Hudson View Poetry Digest x

Summer 2010 (volume 5 number 2)

The two poems were published in A Hudson View Poetry Digest, Summer 2010 (volume 5 number 2) and deals with two themes that appear contradictory at the surface but depend on each other when explored. The first poem though the imagery of smoke opens up a discussion on the illusions that one feeds the mind and be it the lies or the truth, everything gets mixed and creates a fuzziness which fills the space that we constantly create in our lives – the theme of the second poem. We keep creating never ending spaces in our lives for numerous people who enter/leave.
